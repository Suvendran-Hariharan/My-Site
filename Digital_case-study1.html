Land Rover Sri Lanka

To create a stronger connection with Sri Lankan audiences, we tailored Land Rover’s global content for Sinhala and Tamil New Year and Eid, enhancing engagement and growing our follower base.

Objective

Land Rover aims to create a deeper connection with the Sri Lankan audience while still keeping its global image of adventure and rugged luxury intact. The goal is to make the brand feel more relevant locally by aligning with the cultural values and interests of Sri Lankans, positioning Land Rover as the go-to choice for exploration and premium travel experiences.

Social Media Content Strategy

To achieve this, Land Rover’s content strategy focuses on blending global assets with local flavor. By adapting content for key Sri Lankan holidays like Sinhala and Tamil New Year and Eid, we ensure the brand feels connected to local culture. Scenic imagery of Sri Lanka’s landscapes reinforces Land Rover’s adventurous spirit, while interactive posts and collaborations with influencers drive engagement. The strategy aims to make Land Rover a lifestyle brand that resonates with local audiences, while still maintaining its global prestige.
