Magazine Cover Design

Great Places to Work in Sri Lanka 2024

This cover highlights vibrant, collaborative teamwork, symbolizing the thriving workplace cultures of Sri Lanka's top companies, celebrated in the Great Places to Work 2024 rankings.

Idea Behind the Cover

The cover design visually captures the essence of the Great Place to Work® initiative, showcasing employees collaborating in an energetic and open workspace. The bold, large typography "Great Places to Work" represents the strong, impactful workplace cultures found within the top companies in Sri Lanka. The red and white color scheme reflects vibrancy and enthusiasm, symbolizing the dedication of both employers and employees in creating high-performance, trust-filled environments. The overhead view of individuals working together conveys inclusivity, teamwork, and the shared sense of purpose that defines these exceptional workplaces recognized in the 2024 rankings. This cover celebrates the power of collective success.
