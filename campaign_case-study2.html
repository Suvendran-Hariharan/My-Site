Land Rover Extended Warranty

The Land Rover Extended Warranty campaign engaged existing customers, promoting loyalty through targeted emails and informative leaflets on warranty benefits.

Campaign Strategy

The campaign leveraged digital and physical channels to maximize reach. Emails provided direct, personalized outreach, while the printed leaflet reinforced the message with visually engaging details on the extended warranty’s coverage and value, targeting the specific needs of Land Rover owners.

Visual Impact and Campaign Results

The clean, premium design of the leaflet aligned seamlessly with Land Rover’s brand, effectively communicating key warranty benefits. The clear layout made the offer’s value immediately understandable, emphasizing peace of mind, vehicle longevity, and protection. As a result, over 15% of targeted customers updated their extended warranty, with many others gaining awareness of the program’s advantages.
