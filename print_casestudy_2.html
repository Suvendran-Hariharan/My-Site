"Time Traveller Wanted" Vacancy Ad

This ad for Inqube's Group Manufacturing Director role, advertised on LinkedIn and in newspapers, seeks a visionary leader to guide the future of manufacturing innovation in the apparel industry.

Idea Behind the Vacancy Ad

The Time Traveller Wanted ad concept was designed to attract bold, visionary leadership for the role of Group Manufacturing Director at Inqube. The futuristic imagery of a figure stepping through a glowing portal symbolizes the need for a forward-thinking leader capable of navigating future industry challenges. The sci-fi aesthetic conveys innovation, aligning with Inqube’s cutting-edge approach to manufacturing. Advertising on both LinkedIn and in newspapers ensured wide reach, targeting both tech-savvy professionals and experienced leaders across sectors. The ad emphasizes a call to action for candidates ready to lead with a global, future-focused mindset in the evolving world of apparel.
