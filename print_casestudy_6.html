Home of the Geckos

A 90-foot gecko-themed illustration for OSC Sri Lanka's basketball court, celebrating school spirit and athleticism.

Objective

To design a large-scale, 90-foot-long illustration for OSC Sri Lanka’s basketball court, centered on their gecko mascot. The project involved creating a cohesive, vibrant scene across five separate panels, each featuring geckos engaging in various sports activities. The aim was to enhance the court’s atmosphere with a dynamic, school-spirited visual that aligns perfectly across panels, delivering a seamless and energetic backdrop for students and visitors. This required close collaboration with the printing team to ensure precise edge alignment and consistency across all panels.

Impact

The "Home of the Geckos" banner has become a dynamic visual centerpiece on the OSC basketball court, boosting school spirit and creating an engaging environment. The design, crafted with attention to edge alignment across multiple panels, reinforces the school's identity and celebrates athleticism in a fun, memorable way.
