Brochure Design

The Wonder Dehiwala, ocean-inspired

This brochure design for The Wonder apartments embraces an ocean-inspired theme, featuring detailed floor plans and amenities that reflect the charm of this seaside residence in Dehiwala, Sri Lanka.

Idea Behind the Design

The brochure for The Wonder apartment complex was designed with a strong ocean theme to capture the serene, coastal vibe of its seaside location in Dehiwala, Sri Lanka. Inspired by the colors and fluidity of the ocean, the brochure incorporates calming blue tones and wave-like elements to evoke a sense of tranquility. The floor plans and unit layouts were meticulously drawn to scale, providing potential buyers with a clear understanding of the space. The design also highlights local amenities and conveniences, ensuring a comprehensive look at the modern, oceanfront lifestyle that The Wonder offers. The aesthetic emphasizes luxury and coastal living.
