Brand Identity

Book for you

"Book for You" is a brand identity project focused on creating a welcoming and engaging visual identity for a personalized book delivery service.

Idea Behind the Logo

The "Book for You" logo concept revolves around creating a visual that symbolizes accessibility and knowledge. The open book icon emphasizes personalized reading experiences, while the bold typography reflects confidence and clarity. The design aims to balance simplicity and meaningfulness to resonate with a diverse audience of readers.

The Challenge

The main challenge was to create a logo that was both minimalistic and meaningful. It needed to convey the brand’s core value—personalized reading experiences—while remaining simple enough to be recognizable and effective across both digital and print media.

The Solution

We crafted a clean, modern logo featuring an open book to symbolize knowledge and personalization. The minimalist design was paired with a straightforward font and a distinctive color palette, ensuring it remained visually appealing, easy to reproduce, and meaningful across various touchpoints.
