Magazine Cover Design

30 years of LMD

This cover marks 30 years of LMD, symbolizing teamwork and gender equality, with male and female hands collaboratively assembling gears to highlight unity, progress, and inclusivity.

Idea Behind the Cover

The cover design illustrates teamwork and gender equality through hands—male and female—assembling gears, symbolizing the collaborative effort behind LMD’s 30-year journey. The central bronze gear, featuring "30," highlights the milestone of three decades of progress. The contrasting colors emphasize balance between the past and future, with the hands representing equal contributions from both genders in building success. This cover underscores LMD's values of inclusivity, unity, and continuous evolution.
